Our Diploma Courses

Diploma in Battery Technology for EVs

  • In-Depth Battery Knowledge: Provides in-depth knowledge of various battery chemistries used in electric vehicles.
  • Practical Battery Testing: Includes practical sessions on battery testing, maintenance, and performance optimization.
  • Emerging Technologies: Covers the latest advancements in battery technology, including solid-state batteries and fast-charging solutions.
  • Industry Networking: Facilitates networking opportunities with professionals in the battery manufacturing and research sectors.

Diploma in Electric Vehicle Design and Manufacturing

  • Design Principles: Focuses on the principles of electric vehicle design, considering aerodynamics, materials, and safety.
  • Manufacturing Processes: Explores manufacturing processes specific to electric vehicles, including assembly line considerations.
  • Prototyping Projects: Involves students in prototyping projects to apply design principles and manufacturing techniques.
  • Industry Tours: Organizes visits to electric vehicle manufacturing facilities for a firsthand understanding of the production process.

Diploma in EV Policy and Regulation

  • Legal Frameworks: Provides a comprehensive understanding of national and international legal frameworks for electric vehicles.
  • Policy Analysis: Involves students in analyzing existing policies and proposing strategies for sustainable electric vehicle development.
  • Guest Lectures: Invites experts and policymakers as guest lecturers to provide insights into current and future regulatory landscapes.
  • Internship Opportunities: Offers internship opportunities with governmental agencies or organizations involved in EV policy development.

Diploma in Electric Vehicle Project Management

  • Project Planning: Teaches project planning specific to electric vehicle initiatives, considering timelines and resource allocation./li>
  • Stakeholder Engagement: Emphasizes the importance of stakeholder engagement in the successful execution of electric vehicle projects.
  • Risk Management: Covers risk assessment and management strategies for electric vehicle projects.
  • Capstone Project: Requires students to complete a comprehensive capstone project, applying project management principles to a real-world scenario.
