Our Master training Courses:

Master training in Battery Technology and BMSDiploma in Battery Technology for EVs

  • In-Depth Practical Battery Knowledge: Provides in-depth knowledge of various battery chemistries used in electric vehicles.
  • Practical Battery Modelling &Testing: Includes practical sessions on battery Modelling & testing, maintenance, and performance optimization.
  • Emerging BMS Technologies: Covers the latest advancements in battery Management technology, including solid-state batteries and fast-charging solutions.
  • Industry Networking: Facilitates networking opportunities with professionals in the battery manufacturing and research sectors.

Master training in Electric Vehicle Design and Manufacturing:

  • Industrial Design Principles: Focuses on the science and technologies of electric vehicle design, considering aerodynamics, materials, and safety.
  • Industrial Manufacturing Processes: Explores manufacturing and fabrication processes specific to electric vehicles, including assembly line considerations.
  • Industrial Prototyping Projects: Involves students hands-on in prototyping projects to apply design principles and manufacturing techniques.
  • Industry Tours: Organizes visits to electric vehicle manufacturing facilities for a firsthand understanding of the production process, and other industrial activities.

Master class in EV Policy and Regulation

  • Legal Frameworks: It is very important to understand the legal sides of Electric Vehicles so we Provides a comprehensive understanding of national and international legal frameworks for electric vehicles.
  • Policy Analysis: During the course we Involves students in analyzing existing policies and proposing strategies for sustainable electric vehicle development.
  • Guest Lectures: we Invites experts and policymakers as guest lecturers to provide insights into current and future regulatory landscapes for better understanding.
  • Internship Opportunities: We Offers internship opportunities with governmental agencies or organizations involved in EV policy design and development.

Master training in Electric Vehicle Project Management:

  • Project Planning: Our team Teaches project planning specific to electric vehicle initiatives, startups considering timelines and resource allocation.
  • Stakeholder Engagement: Emphasizes the importance of stakeholder engagement in the successful execution of electric vehicle projects.
  • Risk Management: The team of experts teaches risk assessment and management strategies for electric vehicle projects.
  • Capstone Project: Requires students to complete a comprehensive capstone project, applying project management principles to a real-world scenario.

Master training in Two Wheel Electric Vehicle design and Development

  • Industrial Design Principles: Focuses on the science and technologies of electric vehicle two wheeler design, considering aerodynamics, materials, and safety.
  • Industrial Manufacturing Processes: Explores manufacturing and fabrication processes specific to Two wheeler electric vehicles, including assembly line considerations.
  • Two wheeler live fabrication: Involves students hands-on Fabrication of chassis, Fixing of vehicle components on frame.
  • Battery Pack Design: Under the guidance of our expert trainers student will design the battery pack as per required capacity and will connect it to BMS.
